KMed Journals is a peer-reviewed, open-access platform committed to
fostering innovation and advancing knowledge across medical specialities.
It serves as a trusted forum for researchers, clinicians, academicians, and healthcare professionals to exchange insights, share groundbreaking discoveries, and highlight advancements in the ever evolving field of medicine. With a core focus the journal welcomes high-quality submissions that contribute to the understanding, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases. By evidence-based research, KMed Journals aspires to bridge the gap between science and clinical practice, driving progress in global healthcare.

kmed Journal is an emerging, peer-reviewed, academic open access publisher. Our objective is to create a journal of international standard and to disseminate novel scientific and technical information to all fields of academic study. We publish journals across the entire spectrum of science. We also aim to promote research, identify technological advancement by circulating scholarly and professional content worldwide.
